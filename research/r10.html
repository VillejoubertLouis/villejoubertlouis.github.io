F4Is this the same for all listeners?▾

No — and that turns out to be the clinically important part. Some listeners barely noticed a 4 mm mismatch; others struggled with 1 mm. Group averages hide that range; per-listener random effects in the analysis make it visible.

Practically: personalised mapping isn't a nice-to-have. For music, it's required.
